A different approach to buying mineral interests

LongPoint Minerals is a company focused specifically on the purchase of mineral interests in three key US Basins: the Mid-Continent, Permian and Denver-Julesburg. With a highly experienced management team and decades of oil and gas experience, LongPoint Minerals takes an entirely different approach to working with mineral owners to present an offer that’s right for you. That’s how we find value that others miss.

Building on a reputation of doing what we say we’re going to do, LongPoint Minerals has a standard of working alongside mineral owners with respect and integrity. Choosing to sell your mineral rights is often a big decision. When you work with LongPoint Minerals, you will quickly see we have a commitment to keeping our word. We follow through with a process that is simple and seamless but more importantly makes you, the mineral owner, feel respected and confident in your decision to sell.
