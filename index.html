A different approach to buying mineral interests

LongPoint Minerals takes an entirely different approach to working with mineral rights owners in the Mid-Continental, Permian and Denver-Julesburg basins. Instead of giving blanket valuations based on past sales, we use a proprietary technical process to determine the True Value of your mineral interests. With LongPoint, your mineral rights are valued by geologists and engineers, not sales people. That’s how we find value that others miss.

Building on a reputation of doing what we say we are going to do, LongPoint Minerals is committed to respect and integrity. Choosing to sell your mineral rights is often a big decision. When you work with LongPoint Minerals, you will quickly see we have a commitment to keeping our word. Our simple process makes you, the mineral owner, feel respected and confident while selling mineral, oil and gas rights.
